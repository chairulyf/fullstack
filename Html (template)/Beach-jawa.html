Teluk Asmara Beach

Teluk Asmara Beach, is a hidden paradise on the south coast of Malang, East Java. Soft white sand meets calm waves, creating a calming and romantic atmosphere

Siung Beach

Siung Beach, located in Purwodadi Village, Tepus District, Gunung Kidul Regency, Yogyakarta, is a beach destination that offers unique nature and an enchanting atmosphere.

Timang Island

Timang Island is a charming tourist destination located in Gunungkidul, Yogyakarta, Indonesia. The island is popular because of the suspension bridge that connects it to the mainland
