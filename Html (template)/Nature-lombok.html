Komodo Island

Komodo Island is an exotic destination located in Indonesia, especially among the Nusa Tenggara islands. This island is famous for being home to rare giant lizards

Sembalun Lawang

Sembalun Lawang is a village located at the foot of Mount Rinjani, one of the highest mountains in Indonesia, located on Lombok Island, West Nusa Tenggara.

Mount Rinjani

Mount Rinjani is a majestic and highest volcano on Lombok Island, Indonesia. With a height of 3,726 meters above sea level, this mountain offers spectacular natural views and thrilling adventures for climbers.
